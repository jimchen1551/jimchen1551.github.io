About Me

I am a sixth-year medical student at the School of Medicine of National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. In 2023, I completed a minor in Computer Science, where I specialised in algorithms and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on developing machine learning models that use representation learning on multimodal clinical data to assist in diagnosis, predict personalized prognoses, and recommend appropriate treatments. In daily life, I go by Jim.
